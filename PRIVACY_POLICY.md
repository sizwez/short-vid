# Privacy Policy for Mzansi Videos

**Last Updated:** April 10, 2026  
**Effective Date:** April 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Mzansi Videos ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa and other applicable laws.

**Responsible Party:** Mzansi Videos  
**Information Officer:** To be registered with the Information Regulator  
**Contact:** privacy@mzansivideos.app

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Profile Information**: Username, display name, bio, and avatar.
- **Contact Information**: Email address and phone number (optional).
- **Content**: Videos you upload, comments you post, and messages you send.
- **Payment Information**: Payment method details processed securely via Paystack (we do not store card details).

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Technical Data**: IP address, device type, browser type, operating system.
- **Usage Data**: App usage statistics, features used, time spent, pages visited.
- **Device Data**: Device identifiers for push notification delivery.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide the video sharing and social features of the app.
- To process creator subscriptions and earnings.
- To maintain safety through content moderation and reporting.
- To send push notifications you have opted into.
- To improve our services through analytics.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA)
We process your personal information based on:
- **Consent**: Where you have provided explicit consent (e.g., push notifications).
- **Contract**: Where processing is necessary to provide our services.
- **Legitimate Interest**: For safety, security, and service improvement.
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with South African law.

## 5. Data Retention
- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
- **Content**: Videos and comments retained until you delete them or your account.
- **Usage data**: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months.
- **Payment records**: Retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law.

## 6. Your Rights Under POPIA
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information (via app settings).
- **Objection**: Object to processing of your personal information.
- **Complaint**: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (https://inforegulator.org.za).

## 7. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:
- **Paystack**: For secure payment processing.
- **Supabase**: For database hosting and authentication.
- **Google Analytics**: For anonymized usage analytics.
- **Law Enforcement**: When required by law.

## 8. Security
We use Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only authorized users can access private data. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

## 9. Children's Privacy
Mzansi Videos is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
